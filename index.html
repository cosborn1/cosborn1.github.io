I’m a freelance features writer, specialising in health, travel and lifestyle, with experience in journalism, PR, proofreading, editing, content writing and copywriting.

I have written for national press — both print and digital — including The Sunday Mirror and Surfgirl Magazine, as well as numerous blogs and websites.

Although primarily based in the south of England, I like to travel. I often spend winter and summer seasons living in other countries (Japan and the Balkans among my favourites).

In between international travel, you’ll find me roaming the UK in my homemade campervan, seeking out surfing spots, adventure trails and a good place to write.
